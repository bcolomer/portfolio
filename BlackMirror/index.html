Introduction

The series is a product of Netflix Network and its creator and executive producer is Charlie Brooker. The Black Mirror series has two positive qualities not usually found in other series: First, each episode has a hazardous and, albeit realistic, looks at the relationship between human and technology, and second, each episode has completely separate stories and actors...
